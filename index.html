About Jiobit Map Updates

Regular map updates ensure your Jiobit device provides the most accurate location tracking possible. We recommend checking for updates at least once every 3 months.

Our partnership with NaviSolve provides comprehensive map coverage with frequent updates to reflect new roads, points of interest, and geographic changes.

Update Instructions

  1. Ensure your Jiobit device is charged and within Bluetooth range of your phone
  2. Click the "Update Maps Now" button above
  3. Follow the instructions on the NaviSolve portal
  4. Keep the device connected until the update completes (typically 5-10 minutes)

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I update my maps?

We recommend updating every 3 months or whenever you notice location inaccuracies.

Does the update use my mobile data?

Updates are downloaded over Wi-Fi by default to conserve your data usage.

What if my update fails?

Try restarting both your phone and Jiobit device, then attempt the update again. If problems persist, contact support.
